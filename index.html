About Hindustan

Excellence Since 1985

Hindustan Institute of Technology & Science(HITS) is a leading prestigious and recognized institution in India, located at Chennai, Tamil Nadu. It offers a wide spectrum of Undergraduate, Postgraduate, and Doctoral Programs.

HITS is recognised as a Deemed to be University by UGC. The technical courses are approved by AICTE. We have achieved Tier 1 category accreditation from NBA for multiple engineering programs.

Rankings: NIRF has ranked us in the 151–200 band overall. We are consistently ranked among the top private engineering institutions in India.

About IEEE

Advancing Technology for Humanity

IEEE is the world's largest technical professional organization dedicated to advancing technology for the benefit of humanity. IEEE and its members inspire a global community through its highly cited publications, conferences, technology standards, and professional and educational activities.

The IEEE Student Branch at HITS provides a platform for students to grow technically and professionally, offering access to cutting-edge research and a global network of experts.
